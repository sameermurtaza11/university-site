About Us

Our Mission

KINPOE is dedicated to advancing knowledge and fostering innovation in nuclear science and engineering. We strive to educate future leaders, conduct groundbreaking research, and contribute to the responsible development and application of nuclear technologies for the benefit of society.

Our Vision

To be a world-renowned center of excellence in nuclear science and engineering, recognized for impactful research, exceptional educational programs, and commitment to addressing global challenges through nuclear innovation.

Campus Information

Located in the heart of Karachi, our campus provides a vibrant and supportive environment for learning and research. Our state-of-the-art facilities include advanced laboratories, research reactors, and collaborative workspaces designed to foster innovation and discovery.

History

Founded in 1993, KINPOE has a rich history of pioneering research and education in nuclear science and engineering. From our early contributions to nuclear power development to our current focus on advanced nuclear technologies and applications, we have consistently been at the forefront of the field.
